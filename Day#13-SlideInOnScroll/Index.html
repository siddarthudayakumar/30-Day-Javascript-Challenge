Why I Travel

Travelling is something I do in my spare time. Why do I even travel? Soul searching? maybe. Exploring different places? Yeah, sure. Meeting new people and gaining new experiences? Of course! But of late, I have been travelling mostly for soul searching and exploration.

So when did I start to travel? My love for travel started pretty young thanks to my mom and dad taking me to different places after I was born. Earliest trip I remember? An aquarium in a travelling fair in Coimbatore where I was scared by a Lion Fish in a tank.

Of course, there were always the local trips but yeah.

The next huge trip was to Andaman and Nicobar Islands way back in 1995. That seems like a long way off but actually yeah, it was a long back but I still remember the place. Getting terrified by Bats at Viper Island, Pit Viper warning boards and a Helicopter trying to land on Ross island but failing to land and returning to base due to heavy updrafts. The beautiful white sands on Ross Island, the boats with plexiglass bottoms. Sigh. What a beautiful place..

This started a slew of annual trips to different places in India and oddly enough, the only places I haven't travelled to much include Goa, Mumbai and the remote North East. Been to Manali, heck yes. been to Leh? oh yeah! Jim Corbett? yep but sadly no tigers. Maybe Bandipur will be more useful for me tiger-sighting

After leaving India for the US, I have travelled quite a lot over the course of my stay here in the US. Which place have I travelled to a lot? California obviously and New York! New York, you absolute gorgeous city! I felt more at home in New York than in any other place and I tell you, that city can inspire any person down on luck and doing some soul searching. I had the opportunity to travel to the city twice. Yep.
